Tips

    Click on the Buttons

  1. Clicking 'C' tells you the cost of the upgrade.
  2. Clicking 'Space' on some buttons, like the 'Earn' button, might be an upgrade.
  3. Clicking 'P' on clicker buttons tells you how many Joel Tokens you get on each click.
  4. Clicking 'B' on upgrades tells you how many times you used its function.
  5. Don't feel insulted by the alert messages——everyone will get them.
